What is Shaastra Relaunch?
Shaastra, the annual technical festival of Indian Institute of Technology, Madras (IITM), is one of India's largest completely student organised and ISO certified fests. Shaastra Relaunch is a two-day event (happening this 5th and 6th of January 2019) and focusses on solving the enormous but surmountable problem of helping women professionals to get back to their career after a hiatus.

What has Shaastra Relaunch in store for you?
